    • NO RUNNING IN THE HALLS

    Featuring multiple game modes such as:

    Classic:

    Maze of hallways, must enter rooms to find the key to the exit so you can escape.
    There’s a chance the chef is in any room you enter.
    If the chef sees you, he will morph into hunting mode and chase you.
    Don’t get caught running.

    Hard Mode:

    The chef is onto you from the start.
    Constant pursuit, but don't let a teacher catch you running.

    Chef Mode:

    The student has pilfered your precious tacos!
    Play as the chef, hunt down the student before they escape.
    If the student escapes, you die without the sacred sustenence of tacos.

    Infinite:

    One very long hallway, no exits.
    The objective is just to see how far you can make it.
    If a teacher catches you running you die in a very interesting way.

    And Death Animations such as:

    Thousands of pigs the size of ants, A swarm of demon winged corgis at the command of the Queen,
    Bootleg Godzilla, Crazed Doctor, Wizard storm, & a swarm of tiny Sumo wrestlers with centipede legs
