Index of Referenced Planets

Cname is the classification name of the planet/universe
Cname naming instructions
Universe Naming Instructions
Ring - number(s), 0-infinity, 0 is the outer ring
Universe - number(s), 0-total number of universes on that ring, ordered by discovery
*hypen between Ring # and Universe #
Example: 53-19
Planet Naming Instructions
Star Sector - letter(s), case sensitive (aX is different from AX)
Planet - number(s), assigned by order of discovery in that Star Sector
*hyphen between Star Sector and Planet #
Example: Xi-48
*put a colon between universe and planet for full cname (EXAMPLE CNAME: 5-49:ci-5)
