    Basic Description: mythology based fantasy

    This is my personal project (me = Grafton). Mythology has always captured my imagination. But don't you ever get tired of seeing the same pantheons used over and over again? Don't get me wrong, Norse and Greek mythology are fantastic, I just think fantasy is a litte over-saturated with them right now. So that's where I got the idea to base Ikkuna on Baltic and Slavic mythology. Personally, I've always been fascinated by these parts of the world.

    My goals in writing Ikkuna was to bring a world to life; to create a tangible, so-real-you-can-taste-it world and atmosphere.
